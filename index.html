RT Hospital Management System

Real Time Hospital Management System

When an accident happens on the road, an Ambulance arrives, the emergency medical technician takes the photograph of the patient and uploads it on the application, and starts the primary treatment.

Then he scans the finger print of the patient and uploads the information on the application.

App will find the best hospital by following parameters: 1. The smallest distance to the hospital from the place of the accident,
2. The review of hospital, 3. Traffic in the route.

After deciding the hospital, the application automatically calls the decided hospital for the required things for the treatment of the patient. Also, inform his/her family.
